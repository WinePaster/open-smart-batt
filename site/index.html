OpenSmartBatt 是一個由硬體車主自發維護的開源監看客戶端，透過藍牙與您已購買的 RCE 低碳動能智慧電容／電池通訊，讓原廠停業後仍能繼續監看與設定自有設備。

OpenSmartBatt is a community-maintained open-source monitoring client that talks over Bluetooth to the RCE low-carbon smart capacitor/battery you already own, so owners can keep monitoring and configuring their own hardware after the vendor shut down.
