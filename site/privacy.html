總則

OpenSmartBatt 不蒐集、不儲存、不傳輸任何個人資料。本 App 為離線工具，所有資料只留在您自己的裝置上。

App 的資料處理

App 產生的資料（已儲存裝置、遙測歷史、設定、診斷日誌）皆僅儲存在您裝置本機的資料庫，不會上傳到任何伺服器，我們也無法存取。

藍牙（Bluetooth）僅用於與您自己的電池／電容硬體通訊，不用於定位、不用於追蹤，也不蒐集廣告識別碼。

App 會向 GitHub 檢查是否有新版本（僅讀取公開的 release 資訊）；此過程不傳送任何可識別您身分的資料。

本 App 不含任何第三方分析、廣告或追蹤 SDK。

本網站

本網站為靜態頁面，不設任何表單、不蒐集 email、不使用追蹤 Cookie。iOS 的 TestFlight 加入透過 Apple 的 TestFlight 服務完成，適用 Apple 的隱私政策；我們不經手您的 email。

您的權利

由於我們未持有您的任何個人資料，無資料可供查詢、更正或刪除。若要移除 App 產生的本機資料，於裝置上解除安裝 App 即可。

聯絡

有隱私或授權相關問題，請至 GitHub Issues 提出。

Summary

OpenSmartBatt collects, stores, and transmits no personal data. The app is an offline tool; all data stays on your own device.

Data handling in the app

Data the app creates (saved devices, telemetry history, settings, diagnostic log) is stored only in a local database on your device. It is never uploaded to any server and we cannot access it.

Bluetooth is used solely to communicate with your own battery/capacitor hardware — not for location, not for tracking, and no advertising identifiers are collected.

The app checks GitHub for a newer release (reading public release info only); this sends no data that identifies you.

The app contains no third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.

This website

This site is static: no forms, no email collection, no tracking cookies. Joining the iOS TestFlight happens through Apple's TestFlight service under Apple's privacy policy; we never handle your email.

Your rights

Because we hold none of your personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our side. To remove the app's local data, uninstall the app from your device.

Contact

For privacy or licensing questions, please open a GitHub issue.
